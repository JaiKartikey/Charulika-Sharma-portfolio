(01) — About

I study the forces that redraw the maps of global commerce — tariffs, treaties, and the strategic logic shaping nations.

My work at IIFT Delhi sits at the intersection of game theory and empirical trade economics — translating complex policy shifts into tractable models, peer-reviewed publications, and presentations across three continents.
